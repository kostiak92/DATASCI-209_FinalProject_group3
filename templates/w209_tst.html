How to Use the Graphs

Left Graph: Top Restaurants by Restaurant Type or State

  • Each colored dot represents a restaurant, categorized by type/state.
  • Hover over a point to view rating, review count, and type.
  • Ctrl + Click on a point to open its location in Google Maps.

Middle Graph: Filtering by Brush Select

  • Click and drag to draw a selection box ("brush").
  • This filters restaurants based on User Rating and Review Count.
  • Use this to focus on high-rated or frequently reviewed restaurants.
  • Ctrl + Click on a point to open its location in Google Maps.

Right Graph: Brush Display

  • Displays only the data points selected from the middle graph.
  • Use this view to analyze the filtered selection in detail.
  • Ctrl + Click on a point to open its location in Google Maps.
